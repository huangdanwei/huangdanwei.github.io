LAW Mei Ting









MSc Student (Alumna)

Currently Research Associate at Earth Observatory of Singapore

Academic History

MSc National University of Singapore (2022)
Thesis: Light limitation and coral mortality in urbanized reef communities due to sea-level rise
BSc University of London (2020)

Research Interests

Coral reefs and sea-level rise, marine biodiversity and conservation, science communication and community engagement

Master’s Thesis

My project aims to understand the impacts of sea-level rise on turbid and urbanized reef coral communities in Singapore by profiling the depth of corals in relative to future climate change scenarios. Through this study, we can better understand corals resilience and mortality rates under projected sea-level rise in Singapore.

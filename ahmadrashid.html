Ahmad Rafiuddin Rashid

Research Assistant (2016-2017)

Current Project

Enhancing Singapore's Coral Reef Ecosystem in a Green Port

This project explores concepts of sustainable port development in relation to enhancing coral establishment in Singapore’s degraded reefs and non-reef areas, by improving our scientific understanding of the long-term processes necessary for coral reef restoration.

Academic History

B.Sc. (Hons.) in Life Sciences (with specialisation in Environmental Biology), National University of Singapore (2016)
Honours Project: Catchment-scale analysis of tropical land use change and its downstream effects on coral reefs.

Research Interests

Coral reef restoration, coral reef ecology, marine spatial ecology, ecological modelling
